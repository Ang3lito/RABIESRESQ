RabiesResQ Data Privacy Statement

1. Data We Collect

RabiesResQ collects personal and health-related information necessary to create user accounts, manage appointments, and maintain clinical case records.

2. Purpose of Processing

Your information is processed for legitimate healthcare and system operations, including patient registration, case management, vaccination monitoring, and care coordination.

3. Access and Protection

Access to records is limited to authorized users through role-based access controls. RabiesResQ implements reasonable organizational and technical safeguards, including secure authentication and activity logging.

4. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill healthcare, legal, and operational requirements, and is handled in accordance with applicable data privacy obligations.

5. Consent

By creating an account, you acknowledge this Privacy Notice and consent to the collection and processing of your personal data for the purposes stated above.

6. Contact
